Practicum City Municipal Services Online

Dear citizens of Practicum City. During this difficult for us all time we are doing our best to provide you with municipal services.

Please use this online aid to submit requests to some of the City Hall office departments.

  • Senior Citizens

  • Pet owners

  • Construction

  • Social events

  • Low income households

  • Child Development Unit

Please fill in your full name and personal ID number. Scan or make a photograph of your documents and attach them to the form. Acceptable file formats are PDF, JPG or JPEG.

Your request has been received by the service and will be reviewed within seven calendar days. A message about the decision on your request will be sent to your email. You will also receive supporting certificates that have the legal force of an ordinary paper document. Take care of yourself.
